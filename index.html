Superheaven - Alternative Rock

Formerly known as Daylight, Superheaven is an Americal Alternative Rock band formed in 2008.

Originating from Doylestown, Pennsylvania, this band is known for their grunge and shoegaze genre.

In attempts to reintroduce themselves after the "disbandment" of daylight, they published their first album, which is Life in a Jar, with the new name, in 2014
